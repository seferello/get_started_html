The Traveler

My name is Sam. I am a young professional, activist, and traveler working towards my next big move.
I was born and raised in New England, studied vocal performance and Anthropology, and currently work as a proposal writer The next few years will bring many changes as I move to Spain for my Master's in Corporate Communication, travel Europe, and work abroad.



Where I'm Going

I have only had the opportunity to experience a small percentage of our world. There are still many places to go and people to meet! In the next few years I will be moving abroad and seeing as much f my surroundings as time, energy, and bank account will allow.
